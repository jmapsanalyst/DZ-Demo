#Irish DZ-Demo
##An initial demo of Decarbonisation Zones of Ireland, a work in progress. 
Boundaries, be it county, electoral divisions or small census areas were pulled together from a variety of publicly available sources, primarily reports from individual Irish County Council websites. 
Some boundaries conform to small census areas while others have arbitrary delimitations. 
In this case image/raster files have been geolocated to as accurate as possible considering the quality of the images within County Council reports.
